Design

Editorial Design

A booklet designed for the Visual Arts School at the University of Costa Rica with the objective of promoting all the different bachelor's degrees offered in the school, and help people interested in them get all the information needed in order to apply.

The book included a lot of text regarding courses, scholarships and faculty members, and it had to balance out all the information with a heavy use of images to convey the visual aspect of the school.

Illustration

Illustrations and layout design for the book "The Clematis".

The book tells the grim tale of a man who lost his family, so the illustrations were kept in black and white, with a heavy focus on the line art.

A focalized red color was used to highlight the most important elements in the illustrations and help guide the reader through the symbolism of the story.

Iconography

A series of icons designed for an exhibition of products manufactured by indigenous people in Costa Rica.

Each icon represents a different category of product, and it was meant to guide the user through all the arts and crafts offered in the exhibition.
