Development

Data visualization

The Mathematics of AI: A series of exercises developed with the library d3.js.

The data used is a categorization of the different areas and concepts of mathematics that have applications in machine learning and data science. It's objective is to be used as a guide when deciding what subjects in mathematics are the most beneficial when getting into AI.

Hogar Excelencia Familiar

As part of the Web Development curriculum, we developed a site for Hogar Excelencia Familiar. An ONG that provides shelter and support to abandoned and at-risk children and teenagers.

The site can be viewed here.

Design and Development

The site had to handle a lot of information, and in order to balance it out, a lot of photographs of the facilities and the children were added as well.

Some other visual elements like textures were added to help convey warmth. And the colors and icons were designed as a way to add playfulness.

The site was built using the CMS Drupal, so that the owners could easily edit the content and images.

CoversABLE

A landing page for the social collective ConversABLE.

The main objective of the site was to give information on the services and courses offered by the collective, as well as general information about the members.

Besides the static information, the site also has a blog feature that the owners can edit easily on a CMS.
