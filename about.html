About me

Hello!

I’m Irene, a graphic designer and front-end developer from Costa Rica.

But before any of that, I was a mathematician, one that enjoyed racking her brain thinking about the complex problems of pure mathematics, but wanted to bring that way of thinking to other areas and other people as well.

While looking for another place, I went into art school and specialized in graphic design. I loved how design, just like math, can be thought of as problem solving, and both require a lot of creativity to be able to do it.

I then got into web design, and eventually web development, a field that has kept my attention and love for a while, since I’ve found that there’s no limit to what you can accomplish with code.

Something that I enjoy a lot is looking for ways to connect areas and tools that don’t seem to go together, and I’ve found in creative coding and algorithmic design a place where I can use my love for math, design and programming all together.

I’m very excited about the time we are living in, a time where technology is easily accessible and every day people come up with new and amazing ways to use it.

I hope I can be a part of it.
